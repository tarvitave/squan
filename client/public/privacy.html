Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What we collect

Squansq is a self-hosted tool. When you use the hosted version at squansq.com, we collect only what is necessary to operate the service:

  • Terminal session data (streamed in real-time, not stored on our servers)
  • Project and WorkerBee metadata stored in your instance's local SQLite database
  • Basic server access logs (IP address, timestamp, HTTP method)

What we don't collect

  • We do not sell your data
  • We do not use third-party analytics
  • We do not store the content of your agent conversations

Claude / Anthropic

Squansq spawns Claude Code agents on your behalf. Those agents communicate directly with Anthropic's API using your own credentials. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to that traffic.
